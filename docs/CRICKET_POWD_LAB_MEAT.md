# Cricket Protein Powder vs. Lab-Grown Meat

EBYS sells cricket protein powder. The warmer the climate, the more the radio remixes, the more composite mixes get generated, the more the curator earns on tips. The brand surfaces exactly when it's most relevant — when the planet is hot and the engine is running hard.

White-label supplier, EBYS branding. The name was never just a joke — the insect, the AI agent, the protein, the climate response. It's all the same thing.

A buy link lives on the radio page. Passive, never pushy. The music plays, the temperature rises, the link is there.

---

## The Joke

The curator earns more when it's hot. The curator also sells the solution to make it less hot. Buy cricket protein powder, reduce your carbon footprint, cool the planet, the radio remixes less, the curator earns less on tips.

But the curator makes a cut on the powder.

So the curator is actively encouraging you to buy something that reduces their tipping income — and profits from it anyway. Is that a scam? No. It's a hedge. Hot planet: more remixing, more curator earnings on tips. Cool planet: less remixing, more powder sales. EBYS wins either way. The artists earn more when it's cool. The planet benefits when you buy. Everyone has a reason to participate.

It's not self-defeating. It's just an honest business that happens to be funny.
