# Cricket Protein Powder Company

A company that sells cricket protein powder and distributes a share of its revenue to CRKT holders — artists and DJs who earned tokens through the EBYS ecosystem.

No membership. No obligations. No contract. You hold CRKT, you receive a share of powder revenue. You sell your CRKT tomorrow, you're done. You owe nothing and nobody owns you.

The company is support infrastructure for artists. Not a label, not a grant, not a platform taking a cut. A food business that exists in the background and sends money to people making music.

---

## The Sales Force

Artists and DJs are the sales force. They don't know it, and they don't have to.

A DJ performs with EBYS. Their audience watches. The radio page has the buy link. The instrument is named after eating bugs. The brand travels with every performance, every set, every stream — carried by someone who genuinely loves the music, not a marketing team. No pitch. No commission breath. Just the music and the name.

Artists submit tracks. The radio plays them. Listeners discover the music and the brand at the same time — they're the same thing. Some buy powder. Revenue flows back to CRKT holders, including the artist whose track was playing.

The sales force scales automatically with the community. More artists, more DJs, more audiences, more powder exposure. The community grows, the revenue grows, the distributions grow.

---

## CRKT — The Revenue Share

CRKT is how the company measures your share of its revenue. Hold CRKT, earn from powder sales. Nothing more is required.

```
How you get CRKT:
- Artists:   tracks play on EBYS radio → listeners tip → CRKT flows from tip split
- Curators:  perform with EBYS → listeners tip → CRKT flows as curator share
- Anyone:    buy CRKT on the open market
- EBYS:      pre-mined founding allocation
```

```
How CRKT pays out:
Powder sells → revenue split → distribution pool → flows to all CRKT holders monthly
Each holder receives: (their CRKT / total supply) × distribution pool
```

Distribution is automatic and on-chain. It arrives. You don't claim it.

## The Structure

CRKT holders have no say in how the company is run. That's intentional. Artists are free — they submitted music, earned an asset, receive income. They didn't sign anything. They don't attend meetings. They don't vote on powder suppliers.

EBYS runs the company. It manages the white-label supplier relationship, the radio infrastructure, the CRKT token mechanics, and publishes all business metrics publicly — powder sales, listener counts, tipping activity, CRKT distributions. Everything visible, nothing hidden.

EBYS holds a founding CRKT allocation and earns from distributions alongside everyone else. It is not extracting value above the system — it participates in the same economy it built.

## The Climate Connection

The EBYS remixing engine runs hotter when today's temperature exceeds the 10-year rolling average for that date. More heat → more composite mixes → more curator earnings from tips.

The company sells a product that reduces carbon footprint. Hot planet: more remixing, more tips, more curator income. Cool planet: less remixing, more powder sales, more distribution income. Either way, CRKT holders benefit.

The artists earn more when it's cool. The planet benefits when you buy. The DJ earns either way. It's not self-defeating — it's a hedge that happens to be honest.

## Distribution Mechanics

**30% of monthly revenue** goes to the CRKT distribution pool. The remaining 70% funds operations — supplier costs, infrastructure, marketing, growth. The percentage is fixed and public. Everyone can see what came in and what went out.

Distribution happens once a month. All powder sales from the month are batched, 30% is allocated to the pool, and the pool is split proportionally across all CRKT holders in a single on-chain transaction. Cleaner than per-sale, predictable for holders, lower overhead.

```
Monthly revenue: $X
Distribution pool: $X × 0.30
Each holder receives: (their CRKT / total supply) × distribution pool
```

As the business grows, the distribution percentage can increase. 30% is the starting floor, not the ceiling.

---

## EBYS Founding Allocation

EBYS holds 15% of the total CRKT supply at launch, established through a pre-mine. It earns monthly distributions on that stake alongside all other holders.

15% is enough to fund ongoing development and keep EBYS financially aligned with the community's success. It is not enough to dominate distributions — an artist with a highly tipped catalogue can close that gap over time.

The 15% does not increase. As more CRKT is earned and minted over the 20-year schedule, EBYS's relative share dilutes naturally. The community grows into ownership.

---

## Escrow and Unclaimed Earnings

Artists who haven't claimed their CRKT yet still earn distributions. Both the CRKT and the monthly distribution accumulate in escrow, tied to their audio fingerprint. When they claim — however long it takes — they receive everything: the tokens and all the powder revenue that accrued while they weren't watching.

No expiry. No fees. The money waits.
